# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## 🔐 General Safety Tips
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
- Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
- Always verify the sender of emails and messages
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities

## 💳 Financial / UPI Safety
- Never share OTP or PIN with anyone
- Do not approve unknown payment requests
- Always check the app and receiver details before sending money

## 🌐 Awareness & Protection
- Check website URLs (https:// and correct domain)
- Keep your devices updated with latest security patches
- Use antivirus software

## 📞 Report Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Helpline Number: 1930
